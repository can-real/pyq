The Core Story & Mission

CANREAL isn't built to be flashy. It is a straightforward, intentionally simple project designed to solve a single, universal college problem: the immense stress of chasing down study materials right before mid-sems and final exams.
We stripped away all the usual internet clutter to give you a minimal, ultra-clean interface where you can access organized previous year university question papers (PYQs) and high-quality PYQs analysis PDFs in a single click. No ads, no sign-ups, no tracking cookies, and absolutely zero distractions when your clock is ticking.



What is Inside Right Now

~UnitWise PYQ analysis: A strategic breakdown showing you exactly which questions are repeated most frequently in each unit, helping you target high-weightage topics first.
~Verified University PYQs: Access clean, direct links to previous semester question papers without dealing with sketchy download pop-ups.
~Video Playlists: Some helpful youtube playlists


A Note on Future Updates

Please note that this platform is a live, ongoing project! I am actively working on the code behind the scenes right now, which means more engineering subjects and branches will be added very soon. If you don't see your specific subject modules up yet, hang tight—they are on the way.



The Team Behind It

To be completely honest, there is no "team" behind this site. It was built from scratch, curated, and is currently maintained by just a single Student trying to make college prep a little less chaotic for everyone.
